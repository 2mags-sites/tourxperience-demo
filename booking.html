Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TrackMan bay rental at TourXperience Wigan

What should I bring to my TrackMan session?

+

Just bring your golf clubs and comfortable athletic clothing. We provide golf balls, tees, and all technology. If you have TrackMan Golf app, download it beforehand to track your progress.

How accurate is TrackMan technology?

+

TrackMan IO offers unmatched accuracy with measurements precise to 1 foot at 100 yards. It's the same technology used by professional golfers worldwide and trusted by major golf tours.

Can beginners use TrackMan effectively?

+

Absolutely! TrackMan data is valuable for all skill levels. Beginners can see immediate feedback on their swings, while advanced players can fine-tune specific aspects of their game.

What's included in my bay rental?

+

Your TrackMan bay rental includes access to TrackMan IO technology, unlimited shots, real-time data analysis, virtual golf courses, practice games, and mobile app integration.

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability. Peak times (evenings and weekends) may require earlier booking, especially near Wigan area.

Do you offer golf instruction with TrackMan?

+

While TourXperience focuses on TrackMan bay rental, we can arrange professional instruction through our partnership with Tour X Golf. Contact us for coaching packages.

What's your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive full refund. Same-day cancellations may incur charges. We understand emergencies and will work with you when possible.
